STEP 4 — Create Helper Columns

Add the following calculated columns.

1. Tenure Years

This numeric column is used for calculations and pivot tables.

=YEARFRAC([@[Hire Date]],IF([@Attrition]="Yes",[@[Exit Date]],TODAY()))

2. Tenure Display

This converts decimal tenure into readable years and months.

Example:

5 Years 4 Months
2 Years 8 Months

=DATEDIF([@[Hire Date]],IF([@Attrition]="Yes",[@[Exit Date]],TODAY()),"Y") & " Years " & DATEDIF([@[Hire Date]],IF([@Attrition]="Yes",[@[Exit Date]],TODAY()),"YM") & " Months"

3. Age Group
=IFS(
[@Age]<25,"Under 25",
[@Age]<=35,"25-35",
[@Age]<=45,"36-45",
TRUE,"45+")

4. Attrition Flag

This converts:

Yes = 1
No = 0

This makes calculations easier.

=IF([@Attrition]="Yes",1,0)
Why Attrition Flag is Important

Example:

Attrition	Attrition Flag
Yes	1
No	0

This allows easy calculations like:

=SUM(tbl hr[Attrition Flag])

5. Risk Level (Logic-Based Prediction)

This column identifies employees who may resign in future.

Logic Used
High Risk

Employees who:

work overtime
have low satisfaction

OR

poor work life balance
low performance rating
Medium Risk

Employees who:

did not get promotion
worked more than 4 years
Low Risk

Everyone else.

Risk Level Formula
=IFS(
AND([@Overtime]="Yes",[@[Job Satisfaction]]="Low"),"High Risk",
AND([@[Work Life Balance]]="Poor",[@[Performance Rating]]<=2),"High Risk",
AND([@[Promotion Last 5Yrs]]="No",[@[Tenure Years]]>4),"Medium Risk",
TRUE,"Low Risk")

STEP 7 — Build Dashboard

Create a new sheet:

The dashboard design is personal preference.

Focus mainly on readability and business insights.

KPI Formulas Used in Dashboard
Total Employees
=COUNTA(tbl hr[Employee ID])
Active Employees
=COUNTIF(tbl hr[Attrition],"No")
Employees Left
=COUNTIF(tbl hr[Attrition],"Yes")
Attrition Rate
=COUNTIF(tbl hr[Attrition],"Yes")/COUNTA(tbl hr[Employee ID])

Format as Percentage.

Average Salary
=AVERAGE(tbl hr[Salary])
Average Tenure
=AVERAGE(tbl hr[Tenure Years])
