STEP 4 — GET UNIQUE CUSTOMERS

Copy unique customer IDs manually OR use:

=UNIQUE(Raw_Data!B2:B31)

Customer names:

=XLOOKUP(A2,Raw_Data!B:B,Raw_Data!C:C)


STEP 5 — CALCULATE TOTAL REVENUE (M)

Formula:
=SUMIFS(Raw_Data!E:E,Raw_Data!B:B,A2)

This gives:

Total money spent by customer


STEP 6 — CALCULATE FREQUENCY (F)

Formula:
=COUNTIFS(Raw_Data!B:B,A2)

This gives:

Number of orders


STEP 7 — FIND LAST ORDER DATE

Formula:
=MAXIFS(Raw_Data!D:D,Raw_Data!B:B,A2)


STEP 8 — CALCULATE RECENCY (R)

Assume analysis date is:

30-04-2024

Put this date in one cell:
Example:

J1 = 30-04-2024

Now calculate Recency:

=$J$1-E2

Meaning:

Smaller number = more recent customer
Larger number = inactive customer


STEP 9 — CREATE RFM SCORES

Now create new columns:

R_Score	F_Score	M_Score


STEP 10 — CREATE RECENCY SCORE

Smaller recency is better.

Formula:
=IF(F2<=30,5,
IF(F2<=60,4,
IF(F2<=90,3,
IF(F2<=120,2,1))))


STEP 11 — CREATE FREQUENCY SCORE

Higher frequency is better.

Formula:
=IF(D2>=5,5,
IF(D2=4,4,
IF(D2=3,3,
IF(D2=2,2,1))))


STEP 12 — CREATE MONETARY SCORE

Higher revenue is better.

Formula:
=IF(C2>=2500,5,
IF(C2>=1800,4,
IF(C2>=1200,3,
IF(C2>=600,2,1))))


STEP 13 — CREATE FINAL RFM SCORE

Create column:

| RFM_Score |

Formula:

=G2&H2&I2

Example:

555
543
211


STEP 14 — CUSTOMER SEGMENTATION

Create column:

| Segment |

Use this formula:

=IF(AND(G2>=4,H2>=4,I2>=4),"High Value",
IF(AND(G2>=4,H2>=3),"Loyal",
IF(G2<=2,"At Risk",
IF(H2=1,"Low Engagement","Regular"))))
